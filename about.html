Activity Rings Pro

Get Activity Rings with advanced metrics like time in heart rate zone, steps and distances. Track progress and set goals daily, weekly, and monthly.

The App

Transform the way you track your health and fitness with Activity Rings Pro! This app brings the familiar activity ring concept with powerful customization, in-depth metrics and weekly/monthly rings.

Key Features:

  • More Activity Ring Metrics: Personalize your rings by choosing from a wide range of metrics including time in heart rate zones, time of moderate exercise, time of vigorous exercise, steps, distance, calories burned, exercise time and more.
  • Time Period Flexibility: Easily monitor your progress towards your fitness goals with daily, weekly, and monthly views.
  • Personalized Rings: Select vibrant colors to match your style and motivation. Choose the number of rings you need.
  • Set Personalized Goals: Define your own goals for each metric and adjust them as you improve. Activity Rings Pro helps you stay challenged and motivated on your fitness journey.
  • Clear Visualizations: The intuitive ring design provides a clear and motivating visual representation of your progress.
  • User-Friendly Setup: Easily configure your rings, choose your metrics, and set your goals with the app's simple and intuitive setup process.
  • HealthKit Integration: Seamlessly connect with Apple HealthKit to import your health data and ensure accuracy.

Activity Rings Pro is designed to keep you motivated and engaged in your fitness journey. Whether you're aiming to increase your daily activity, train for a specific event, or simply maintain a healthy lifestyle, this app provides the tools and visual feedback you need to succeed.
